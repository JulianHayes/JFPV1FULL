About

Brand leader. Business builder. Big-idea believer.
For over 25 years, Justin Fisher has been where brand meets business—and made it brilliant.
He’s led global marketing, shaped creative strategy, and built high-performance teams from scratch.

Justin brings commercial clout, creative courage, and calm, calculated confidence.
He’s worked both sides of the brief—client and agency—so he knows how to lead the work that lands, lasts, and lifts results.

In 2020, he reversed a four-year brand decline at American Express, lifting value by 8% to $19 BN.
From fashion to finance, Zegna to Ziggy Stardust, he turns insights into ideas that sell.
